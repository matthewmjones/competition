Competition Simulation

Understanding the impact of pricing strategies on market dynamics is essential, as pricing decisions influence not only individual profitability but also consumer behaviour, competitive responses, and overall market efficiency. In this competition simulator, you and your peers can experiment with pricing choices and observe how these decisions affect outcomes. The objective is to maximise profit by selecting the price at which you will sell your goods, while navigating the dynamics of competition.
